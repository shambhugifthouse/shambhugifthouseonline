# Shambhu Gift House POS & E-Store

Modular Billing, Inventory, Xerox & Services, Personal Finance, and E-Store Management System.

## Features
- **POS & Billing**: Fast billing, invoicing, and receipt printing.
- **Product & Inventory Catalog**: Stock tracking, categories, low stock alerts.
- **Xerox & Services**: Service rates and printer paper/ink inventory manager.
- **Personal Services & Finance**: Expense management and EMI spending loan tracker.
- **Customers & Suppliers**: Khata ledger and supplier management.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Comprehensive sales and profit analysis.
